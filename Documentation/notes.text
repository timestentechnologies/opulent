About Us
Laundry? Not your thing? We get it.
That’s why Opulent Laundry is here—to take laundry off your to-do list and bring fresh, clean clothes straight to your door.

Whether you’re a student buried in assignments, a parent juggling everything, or a young professional chasing big goals—we’ve got you. No more laundry piles, no more weekend wash-day struggles. Just fresh vibes, clean threads, and effortless convenience.

We pick up, we clean, we deliver. Simple as that. And yes, we’re always on time.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
🧺 GENERAL
Q: What services do you offer?
A: We offer wash, dry & fold, wash, dry, iron & fold, ironing only, pick-up & delivery, and special garment care. We also handle linens, baby clothes, and more.

Q: Do I need to sort my laundry before pick-up?
A: Not at all! We'll take care of sorting by color, fabric type, and washing instructions.

🚚 PICK-UP & DELIVERY
Q: How do I schedule a pick-up?
A: Simply message us on WhatsApp at 0745812730 with your name, location, and preferred pick-up time or via our website

Q: What are your pick-up and delivery hours?
A: We operate Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Let us know what works for you!

Q: Is there a delivery fee?
A: Delivery is free within our service area. For longer distances, a small fee may apply—we’ll inform you upfront.

⏱ TIMING & TURNAROUND
Q: How long does it take to get my laundry back?
A: Standard turnaround is 24 to 48 hours. We also offer same-day express service on request (subject to availability).

Q: Can I schedule weekly or regular laundry service?
A: Yes! Ask about our subscription plans for weekly pick-up and delivery.

👕 CLOTHES & CARE
Q: Do you handle delicate or special fabrics?
A: Absolutely. We use gentle cycles, proper settings, and hand-wash where needed to care for delicates like silk, lace, and baby clothes.

Q: What detergent do you use? Can I request fragrance-free?
A: We use high-quality, skin-friendly detergents. Fragrance-free and hypoallergenic options are available—just let us know when booking.

Q: What happens if something is damaged or missing?
A: We treat every item with care. In the rare case of damage or loss, we follow a fair compensation policy—your satisfaction is our priority.

💳 PAYMENT
Q: How do I pay?
A: We accept cash, mobile money, and bank transfers. Payment is made upon delivery unless otherwise arranged.

Q: Are there discounts for bulk laundry or regular customers?
A: Yes! We offer discounts for bulk orders, students, and weekly plans. Ask us for more details when booking.
